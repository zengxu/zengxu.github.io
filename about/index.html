About Xu Zeng

Hello,

I’m Xu Zeng (Chinese name: 曾旭). I was born in a little town Linchuan of Jiangxi Province of China. When I was 6 years old, I moved to a small city Yingtan with my parents, and spent my elementary, middle and high school there. I got a high score in College Entrance Examination, and then I entranced one of the top universities – Zhejiang University, located in Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province. The City is famous by West lake, a beautiful lake.
　　I started my colleague life in 2003 by entering the Optical Engineering department, which I think is the top one in China. I spent four years to get the Bachelor Degree and then I was recommended to continue the PhD study in the same department. At the beginning, my research interest is optical sensors, more specifically, highly sensitive accelerometer sensors based on the techniques of MOMES, fiber optics and nanophotonics. Then during the year of 2008 - 2009, I got the chance to study in Japan as a visiting researcher, in the Central Research Laboratory of Hamamatsu Photonics K.K.. Where I firstly touched the area of optical spatial light modulator, basically it’s a technique for optical information processing. I utilized it for the application of optical pattern recognition. After one year, I was back to China and continued the research of liquid crystal spatial light modulators. In 2012, I obtained my PhD degree in “Metrology and Measurement Technology and Instruments” from Optical Engineering Department of Zhejiang University and then I joined Philips and started my first job. I joined the Lighting department of Philips Research China and as a research scientist and focused on the innovation development of LED lighting products from optics. My major responsibilities are: optical measurement, non-imaging optics design and lighting application research.
　　The primary purpose of building this blog is to record the thoughts and experiences both in working and life. With the background of optics, most of the content will be optics related.
　　Anything to discuss, please send emails to: optzeng@gmail.com 　　

ENJOY!
